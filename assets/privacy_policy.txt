# QR Vault Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

## Data Collection

QR Vault respects your privacy. Here's what we collect:

### Local Data (Stored Only on Your Device)
- **Scan History**: QR codes you scan are stored locally using Hive database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or sync to cloud.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (theme, sound, vibration) are stored locally.
- **App Usage**: Anonymous analytics to improve the app.

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- If you enable cloud sync, your scan history is uploaded to Firebase Cloud Storage.
- Data is associated with an anonymous account ID.
- You can delete your cloud data anytime by clearing app data.

### Camera Access
- QR Vault requires camera access to scan QR codes.
- We do not take photos or record video.
- Camera frames are processed locally and discarded immediately.

## Data Usage

We use collected data to:
- Provide QR scanning and generation features
- Save your scan history for reference
- Enable optional cloud backup

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Your Rights

- **Delete Data**: Clear all local data from Settings > About > Clear History
- **Disable Cloud Sync**: Turn off sync in Settings
- **Withdraw Consent**: Uninstall the app removes all local data

## Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@example.com

## Children's Privacy

QR Vault is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
